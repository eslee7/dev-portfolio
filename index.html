Language & Immigration

Language and immigration are inextricably intertwined - but how exactly do they affect each other? To find out, I programmed a multi-agent model in NetLogo that can play out millions of immigration scenarios - exploring generational language loss, emergence of bilingalism, and more.

Created for Professor Uri Wilensky, who developed the NetLogo language.
